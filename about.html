About Finch & Poppy Farm

A 5-acre sustainable farm in Stanford, Kentucky

Our Story

Finch & Poppy Farm LLC is a sustainable agricultural enterprise committed to producing organic honey, vegetables, broiler chickens, and multicolored eggs. With a focus on ecological health and community engagement, we integrate apiary, raised gardens, poultry, and vertical farming to create diverse revenue streams including CSA subscriptions, subscription boxes, and corporate programs.

Our Mission

To provide sustainably grown, organic food products while promoting pollinator health and biodiversity. We strive to build a resilient local food system that supports ecological balance and meets the needs of environmentally conscious consumers.
