About Finch & Poppy Farm

A 6-acre sustainable farm in Stanford, Kentucky

Our Story

Finch & Poppy Farm LLC is a sustainable agricultural enterprise committed to producing organic honey, vegetables, broiler chickens, and multicolored eggs. Our current focus is on handcrafted products like small-batch jams, luxury candles, and botanical perfumes, inspired by the farm's botanicals and sold locally. As the farm becomes more functional, we'll expand to integrate these with direct farm offerings.

Our Mission

To provide sustainably grown, organic food products while promoting pollinator health and biodiversity. We strive to build a resilient local food system that supports ecological balance, starting with our handcrafted goods and evolving with the farm's growth.
